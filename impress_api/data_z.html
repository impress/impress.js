data-z


data-z is the tool that controls the forward(-z) and backward(z) movement in the 3rd dimensional position of the step on the infinite coordinate canvas. data-z takes a value of infinity to negative infinity. The location is determined from the center pixel of a four quadrant coordinate system. data-z is often used in conjunction with data-x, and data-y.
